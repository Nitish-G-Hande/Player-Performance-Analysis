About Indian Premier League

  1. The Indian Premier League (IPL) is a professional Twenty20 cricket league in India that is contested by ten teams representing different cities in India. The league was founded by the Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI) in 2007 and has since become one of the most popular cricket leagues in the world 12. The league is played annually and usually takes place between March and May.
  2. IPL has been sponsored by TATA since 2021 and is officially known as TATA IPL. The league has a total of 60 matches, with each team playing 14 matches during the regular season. The top four teams at the end of the regular season qualify for the playoffs, which consist of two qualifiers, an eliminator, and a final.
  3. The IPL has been known to attract some of the best cricket players from around the world, with players from countries such as Australia, England, South Africa, and the West Indies participating in the league. The league has also been known to be a platform for young Indian cricketers to showcase their talent and earn a spot on the Indian national team.
